About Mercedes-Benz

Mercedes-Benz is a German global automobile marque, founded in 1926, and is renowned for producing luxury vehicles, buses, and trucks. It is one of the most admired automotive brands in the world.

With its advanced technology and AMG performance division, Mercedes-Benz offers everything from executive sedans to powerful supercars, redefining luxury mobility.

Key Specifications (Popular Model: Mercedes-AMG GT Black Series)

  • Engine: 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8
  • Horsepower: 720 hp
  • Top Speed: 325 km/h
  • 0-100 km/h: 3.2 seconds
  • Drive Type: Rear-Wheel Drive
  • Transmission: 7-speed dual-clutch automatic
