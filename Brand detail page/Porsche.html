About Porsche

Porsche AG is a German automobile manufacturer specializing in high-performance sports cars, SUVs, and sedans. Founded in 1931, Porsche has become a global icon of performance and luxury.

Known for models like the 911, Porsche combines heritage with innovation, making it one of the most admired automotive brands in the world.

Key Specifications (Popular Model: Porsche 911 Turbo S)

  • Engine: 3.8L Twin-Turbocharged Flat-6
  • Horsepower: 640 hp
  • Top Speed: 330 km/h
  • 0-100 km/h: 2.7 seconds
  • Drive Type: All-Wheel Drive
  • Transmission: 8-speed PDK automatic
