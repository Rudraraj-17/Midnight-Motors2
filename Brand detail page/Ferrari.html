Ferrari

Ferrari is the embodiment of speed, passion, and Italian excellence. Known for crafting some of the world’s most desirable sports cars, Ferrari is a symbol of performance and prestige.

About Ferrari

Ferrari S.p.A. is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939. It is renowned for producing high-performance vehicles with iconic designs and cutting-edge technology.

Ferrari cars are instantly recognizable for their prancing horse emblem and racing legacy. Combining power with artful engineering, Ferrari continues to inspire enthusiasts worldwide.

Key Specifications (Popular Model: Ferrari SF90 Stradale)

  • Engine: 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Hybrid
  • Horsepower: 986 hp
  • Top Speed: 340 km/h
  • 0-100 km/h: 2.5 seconds
  • Drive Type: All-Wheel Drive
  • Transmission: 8-speed dual-clutch automatic
