About Koenigsegg Jesko

The Koenigsegg Jesko, named after Jesko von Koenigsegg, is a Swedish hypercar that pushes the boundaries of automotive engineering. With its lightweight design and track-focused technology, it is built for record-breaking performance.

Combining a powerful engine, active aerodynamics, and futuristic design, the Jesko is not only a hypercar but also a masterpiece of modern engineering.

Key Specifications (Koenigsegg Jesko)

  • Engine: 5.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8
  • Horsepower: 1600 hp (on E85 fuel)
  • Top Speed: 482+ km/h (theoretical)
  • 0-100 km/h: ~2.5 seconds
  • Drive Type: Rear-Wheel Drive
  • Transmission: 9-speed multi-clutch Light Speed Transmission
