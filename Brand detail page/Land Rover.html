About Land Rover

Land Rover is a British brand specializing in four-wheel-drive luxury SUVs, founded in 1948. It is recognized worldwide for its ability to blend adventure-ready durability with refined design.

From the iconic Defender to the luxurious Range Rover, Land Rover vehicles symbolize strength, innovation, and sophistication.

Key Specifications (Popular Model: Range Rover Autobiography)

  • Engine: 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8
  • Horsepower: 523 hp
  • Top Speed: 250 km/h
  • 0-100 km/h: 4.6 seconds
  • Drive Type: All-Wheel Drive
  • Transmission: 8-speed automatic
