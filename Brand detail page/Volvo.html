About Volvo

Volvo Cars is a Swedish multinational manufacturer of luxury vehicles, founded in 1927. It is famous for pioneering automotive safety features and delivering vehicles that emphasize comfort and innovation.

With a strong focus on electrification, Volvo is shaping the future of sustainable driving while maintaining its legacy of premium Scandinavian craftsmanship.

Key Specifications (Popular Model: Volvo XC90 Recharge)

  • Horsepower: 455 hp

  • 0-100 km/h: 5.4 seconds
  • Drive Type: All-Wheel Drive
